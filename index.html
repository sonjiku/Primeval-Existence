About the project...

Primeval Existence is a black metal project, started by Oculus Antiquus and Blasphemus Prophet in Rethymno. The band consisted of Oculus, Blasphemus Prophet, Amnesiac and Unholy Grandpa (of Flesh Martyr and CHAOSHORDE). After recording their first song that was supposed to be realeased, Oculus disbanded the project due to creative differences and the difficulty of all members to put time into it because of life obligations.

After 2 years of inactivity, Oculus decided to continue working on the project in 2020, and started working towards releasing an EP with the previously recorded single, and 3 more songs written by him and some help from the previous members.

Latest Song

Fiend Is the first song ever released by the project. It was recorded in 2017 by Oculus, in his family home in Rethymno. The song has been stayed on a hard drive for over 4 years. It’s finally time for the world to be blessed by the blood gurgling sounds of Oculus’ screams.
